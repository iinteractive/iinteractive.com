Who We Are

Sometimes in our industry, technology buzzwords and jargon overwhelm the human side of the equation. Infinity Interactive, Inc was founded in February 1998 with the goal of enhancing the human side, helping you understand the technical aspects that enable your project to run smoothly.

In a well managed project, technology drives communication. It should always be a means to an end, never the centerpiece itself.

While others have fallen by the wayside pushing the latest technological slogans, buzzwords, and lingo for the sake of technology itself, we have thrived through both economic boom and hardship. We feel this is because of our unwavering commitment to plain spoken, yet technically proficient, communication with our clients.

We continue to provide that essential service to this day.

Methodology

We apply our flexible methodology to every project in order to meet your goals in the most efficient manner possible. Our years of experience have honed our methodology, as well as provided guidance for our project recommendations.

Through the years, we have found that there is not one single technological solution that scales to meet every problem. We offer flexibility, stability, and the assurance that you will receive the appropriate solution for your project.
